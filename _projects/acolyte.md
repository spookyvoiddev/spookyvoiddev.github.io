**Acolyte** is a collaborative tabletop role-playing game. Players take on the roles of Acolytes — apprentice wizards on the cusp of graduation from the **Wizard Academy for New Wizards** — as they set forth on their final quest before becoming proper spellcasters.

- **Role:** Sole designer, writer, layout
- **Year:** 2021
- **Pages:** Short-form rulebook, single-session play
- **Hosted on itch.io:** [hiddendevslug.itch.io/acolyte](https://hiddendevslug.itch.io/acolyte)
