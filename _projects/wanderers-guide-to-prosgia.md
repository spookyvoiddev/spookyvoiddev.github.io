**Wanderer's Guide to Prosgia** is a Dungeons & Dragons 5e supplement introducing new character options, spells, magical items, devious creatures, and an elemental desert setting to experience them in.

I served as **creative lead** on a 10-person team (5 writers, 4 artists, 1 producer). I conceived the project and spearheaded development of the battlemaps, monsters, spells, items, backgrounds, and subclasses.

- **Role:** Creative lead, mission/quest design
- **Team:** 10 people (writers, artists, producer)
- **Year:** 2022
- **Tools:** D&D 5e SRD, Dungeondraft, custom adventure layout
- **Hosted on itch.io:** [hiddendevslug.itch.io/wanderers-guide-to-prosgia](https://hiddendevslug.itch.io/wanderers-guide-to-prosgia)
- **Postmortem:** [Read the development write-up]({% post_url 2022-03-15-wanderers-guide-to-prosgia %})
