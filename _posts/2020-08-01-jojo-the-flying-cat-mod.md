Hail friends! It’s been a while since I have posted and that is mostly due to the current epidemic plaguing our world. Fortunately, I have found the motivation to stay productive through this and I have been hard at work on my next project. Now, if you know anything about me, you know I LOVE my cat Jojo, and I also love Terraria! (I’m sure you can see where this is going.)

Due to my love of both of these things, I decided to try my hand at modding for the very first time, and why not start out by learning how to put in my cute little siamese into an amazing sandbox RPG?

![](/assets/img/posts/tmodloader_2020-08-01_12-09-23-c5f8f6.png)
_The two rascals themselves!_

Jojo has custom AI, which can be found on my [github](https://github.com/Franny-Dancis). The staff is a lazer pointer, and all the art is done by my amazing friend and artist, Sam Mercado, who deserves much more credit than he gets for his amazing work. Without him, this project would’ve easily never reached this point.

I highly recommend anyone modding to anyone who even has a remote interest in programming as it is quite fun, and you can see the fruits of your labor quite quickly, especially in Terraria!

It’s a short post today, but I hope you are all staying safe! Have a good one!
